# RAG Challenge Overview

Welcome to the **IFS RAG Challenge**. This challenge guides you through designing a secure, scalable, and governed Azure-based Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) chatbot for IFS employees, leveraging the Baseline OpenAI E2E Chat reference architecture.

---

## Challenge Objectives

- Analyze IFS’s business context and drivers for a RAG chatbot
- Identify and prioritize business, technical, and compliance requirements
- Design a robust Azure RAG architecture aligned with best practices
- Present and justify your solution to peers

---

## Challenge Structure

This challenge is divided into five steps:

1. [Step 1: Scenario](./ifs-rag-step1-scenario.md) – Analyze the business scenario and user needs
2. [Step 2: Requirements](./ifs-rag-step2-requirements.md) – Identify and document requirements
3. [Step 3: Design](./ifs-rag-step3-design.md) – Design the RAG architecture
4. [Step 4: Integrate](./ifs-rag-step4-integrate.md) – Plan integration with Azure Landing Zones
5. [Step 5: Present](./ifs-rag-step5-present.md) – Present and justify your solution

---

## How to Use This Challenge

- Work as a team to complete each step in order
- Use the provided activities, guidance, and success criteria to structure your work
- Reference the [Azure Best Practices](../05-azure-best-practices/) for proven recommendations
- Document your findings and designs for each step
